Section 05

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), hashed password storage, access control limiting data access to authorised personnel only, and regular security reviews.

Seller identity documents (passport copies, ID scans) are stored securely and accessible only to SariKo compliance staff for KYC verification purposes. They are not shared with other sellers or buyers.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, or as required by Vietnamese law (typically five years for financial records under tax law). You may request deletion of your account data at any time — see Section 10.

Data breach notification: In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify affected users and report to the relevant Vietnamese authorities in accordance with Decree 13/2023 requirements.
Section 06

Cookies and Tracking

SariKo uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the platform. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for login sessions, cart functionality, and secure checkout. Cannot be disabled without breaking platform function.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage data to understand how the platform is used and improve performance. No personally identifiable data is included.

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking cookies, or sell cookie data to any third party. You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Section 07

Your Rights Under Vietnamese Law

Under Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP (Vietnam Personal Data Protection Decree), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Know You have the right to know what personal data SariKo holds about you, why it is held, and how it is used.
  • Right to Access You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days.
  • Right to Correction If any of your personal data is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request correction.
  • Right to Deletion You may request deletion of your personal data. Note: some data must be retained for legal and tax compliance purposes under Vietnamese law and cannot be deleted upon request.
  • Right to Object You may object to processing of your personal data for purposes beyond contract performance or legal obligation (e.g., analytics).
  • Right to Withdraw Consent Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
  • Right to Complain You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Department of Personal Data Protection under Vietnam's Ministry of Public Security if you believe your data rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at poppet@sariko.store or by phone at +84 909 074 793.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

SariKo is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you are under 18, please do not create an account or submit any personal data through the platform.

If we become aware that personal data has been collected from a person under 18 without verified parental consent, we will delete that data promptly. If you believe a minor has registered on our platform, please contact us immediately at poppet@sariko.store.
