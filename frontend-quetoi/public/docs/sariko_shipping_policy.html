Shipping & Delivery Policy

Version 1.0 Effective Date April 19, 2026 Operator Zen Tech Asia Co., Ltd.
This Shipping & Delivery Policy applies to all orders placed through the SariKo platform at www.sariko.store and app.sariko.store. It is issued by Zen Tech Asia Co., Ltd. (MST 0316852554), the operator of SariKo, in compliance with Decree 52/2013/NĐ-CP on E-Commerce and its amendments.
Section 01

Delivery Coverage Area

SariKo currently provides delivery services within Ho Chi Minh City (HCMC), Vietnam only. Delivery is available across all districts of Ho Chi Minh City, subject to delivery provider availability at the time of order.

SariKo does not currently offer delivery outside of Ho Chi Minh City. Buyers with addresses in Hanoi, Da Nang, or other cities outside HCMC are not able to complete purchases requiring delivery at this time. Buyers may arrange self-collection directly with a seller where both parties agree, but this is outside SariKo's platform delivery service.

Note: Delivery availability to specific addresses within HCMC is subject to the real-time availability of Lalamove, GrabExpress, or Ahamove drivers in the seller's area at the time of order confirmation. In the rare event no driver is available, the seller will notify the buyer promptly.

SariKo is a marketplace intermediary. The delivery contract for each order is between the seller and the chosen delivery provider. SariKo facilitates coordination but is not itself a delivery company.

Section 03

Estimated Delivery Times

Delivery times are estimates based on distance between seller and buyer within Ho Chi Minh City, and are subject to traffic conditions, driver availability, and order preparation time. Estimated delivery times are shown at checkout.

Distance / Scenario Estimated Delivery Time Notes
Same district (seller and buyer) 30 – 60 minutes From order confirmation by seller
Adjacent districts (short cross-district) 45 – 90 minutes Includes pickup + transit time
Cross-district (mid-range) 60 – 120 minutes e.g. Binh Thanh to District 7
Outer districts (Thu Duc, Binh Chanh, Nha Be) 90 – 150 minutes Subject to driver availability
Peak hours: Orders placed during peak lunch (11:00–13:00) and dinner (17:00–20:00) periods may experience longer wait times due to higher demand for delivery drivers across HCMC.
Section 04

Delivery Fees

Delivery fees are calculated by the delivery provider (Lalamove, GrabExpress, or Ahamove) based on the distance between the seller's location and the buyer's delivery address. SariKo does not mark up delivery fees — the fee shown at checkout is the provider's fee passed through directly.

SariKo charges zero platform fee on delivery. The delivery fee and the goods total are the only amounts the buyer pays.

Distance Range Estimated Fee (VND) Approx. USD
Short (same / adjacent district) ₫15,000 – ₫25,000 ~$0.60 – $1.00
Medium (cross-district) ₫25,000 – ₫40,000 ~$1.00 – $1.60
Long (outer districts) ₫40,000 – ₫55,000 ~$1.60 – $2.20
General fallback estimate ₫20,000 – ₫50,000 ~$0.80 – $2.00

The exact delivery fee is confirmed by the seller when they accept the order and book the delivery driver. If the actual fee differs from the estimate shown at checkout, the seller will notify the buyer before dispatch.

Delivery fees are non-refundable once the delivery driver has been dispatched, except in cases where the delivery failure is caused by the seller (wrong item, incorrect address provided by seller, or seller cancellation after dispatch).

Section 05

How Delivery Works — Step by Step

  1. Buyer places order Buyer selects items, enters delivery address, and pays via VNPay. Payment is held in SariKo escrow — the seller does not receive payment yet.
  2. Seller confirms order The seller receives a notification and confirms the order, indicating estimated preparation time.
  3. Seller books delivery driver Seller books a Lalamove, GrabExpress, or Ahamove driver through the respective app, using the buyer's delivery address.
  4. Driver picks up from seller The delivery driver collects the order from the seller's location.
  5. Seller shares tracking link Seller pastes the delivery tracking URL into SariKo so the buyer can follow the driver in real time.
  6. Buyer receives order and confirms delivery Once the order arrives, the buyer taps "Confirm Delivery" in the SariKo app. This releases the escrowed payment to the seller. This action is final — confirm only when satisfied with your order.
Section 06

Failed, Delayed, or Damaged Deliveries

Failed delivery: If a delivery driver cannot locate the buyer's address, the driver will contact the buyer by phone. If the buyer cannot be reached, the order may be returned to the seller. SariKo will investigate and determine whether a refund or re-delivery is appropriate on a case-by-case basis.

Delayed delivery: SariKo is not liable for delivery delays caused by traffic, weather events, road closures, or delivery provider issues beyond our control. If a delivery is significantly delayed, the buyer should contact poppet@sariko.store for assistance.

Damaged goods: If goods arrive visibly damaged, the buyer must photograph the damage before accepting delivery and contact poppet@sariko.store within 24 hours of receipt. Do not tap "Confirm Delivery" if goods are damaged — contact SariKo support first.

Important: Once the buyer taps "Confirm Delivery," the transaction is complete and payment is released to the seller. Refund requests after this point require a separate dispute process. Only confirm delivery when you are satisfied with your order.
Section 07

Seller Responsibilities

Sellers on SariKo are responsible for:

  • Preparing orders accurately and within the time indicated when confirming the order
  • Packaging goods appropriately for motorcycle delivery — including secure packaging for food to prevent spillage or damage in transit
  • Booking a delivery driver promptly after order confirmation
  • Providing the correct buyer address to the delivery driver
  • Sharing the delivery tracking link with the buyer via the SariKo platform
  • Notifying the buyer promptly if they are unable to fulfil an order

Sellers who repeatedly fail to fulfil orders, provide inaccurate delivery information, or cause delivery failures through negligence may have their seller accounts reviewed or suspended.
