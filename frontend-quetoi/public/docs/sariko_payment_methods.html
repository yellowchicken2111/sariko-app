Section 02

How Payment Works — The Escrow System

SariKo uses a secure escrow model to protect both buyers and sellers. Your payment is never released directly to the seller at the moment of purchase — it is held in SariKo's escrow account until you confirm delivery.

1
Buyer pays at checkout via VNPay

The full order amount (goods + delivery fee) is processed by VNPay and held in SariKo's escrow account. The seller does not receive payment yet.

2
Seller prepares and dispatches the order

The seller confirms, prepares, and dispatches the order via Lalamove, GrabExpress, or Ahamove.

3
Buyer receives the order

The delivery driver delivers the order to the buyer's address. The buyer inspects the goods.

4
Buyer confirms delivery — payment released

The buyer taps "Confirm Delivery" in the SariKo app. This releases the escrowed payment to the seller. This action is final. Only confirm when satisfied with your order.

5
Seller receives payout on weekly settlement

Confirmed order amounts are batched and paid to sellers every Wednesday for the prior week's settled orders, via the seller's registered payout method.

Auto-confirmation: If you do not confirm or dispute delivery within 48 hours of the scheduled delivery time, the order is automatically marked as delivered and payment is released to the seller. Please check the app promptly after your order arrives.
Section 03

Currency

Currency Policy
All prices on SariKo are displayed and charged in Vietnamese Dong (VND). This is the sole transactional currency on the platform in compliance with Vietnamese foreign exchange regulations.

If you pay with a foreign-issued Visa or Mastercard, your card issuer applies its own USD/VND (or other currency) conversion rate. SariKo has no control over and does not benefit from this exchange rate. We recommend checking your bank's rates before paying with a foreign card.

Section 05

Seller Payout Methods

Sellers receive their weekly settlement payouts via one of the following methods, selected during seller onboarding. Payouts are processed every Wednesday for the prior week's confirmed orders.

Vietnam Bank Transfer
VND Bank Account

Direct bank transfer to any Vietnamese bank account (Techcombank, Vietcombank, MB Bank, BIDV, and others). Processed in VND. No transfer fee charged to seller.

International Transfer
Wise Transfer

For sellers who prefer to receive payouts in Philippine Peso (PHP) or another currency. Processed via Wise Business at approximately 0.5–1% transfer fee, disclosed to the seller at payout time.

Digital Wallet
PayPal

Available for sellers with a verified PayPal account. Processing fees apply per PayPal's standard rates and are disclosed to the seller during onboarding.

Coming Soon
E-Wallet (VN)

MoMo and ZaloPay payout options for Vietnamese sellers will be available in a future update.

Sellers select their payout method during the seller onboarding process at joinna.sariko.store. Payout method changes may be requested by contacting poppet@sariko.store.

Section 06

Refunds

Refund eligibility is determined by the circumstances of the order. The following guidelines apply:

  • Order not fulfilled by seller: Full refund issued to buyer's original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • Wrong item delivered: Full refund or replacement at buyer's discretion, subject to SariKo investigation.
  • Goods damaged on arrival: Refund considered subject to photographic evidence submitted within 24 hours of receipt. Do not confirm delivery if goods are damaged.
  • Buyer confirmed delivery — change of mind: Refunds are not available after the buyer has confirmed delivery. The confirmation action is final.
  • Delivery fee: Non-refundable once the delivery driver has been dispatched, except where delivery failure is caused by the seller.

To initiate a refund request, contact poppet@sariko.store with your order details and the reason for the request. Refunds are processed within 7 business days of approval back to the original payment method.
